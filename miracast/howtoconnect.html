How to Connect

Connecting Your Device

Follow these steps to connect your device with our application:

  1. Step 1: Launch the App

    Open the application on your device and go to the connection settings.

  2. Step 2: Choose Your Device

    Select the device you wish to connect to from the available options.

  3. Step 3: Confirm Connection

    Confirm the connection request on both your device and the selected device.

  4. Step 4: Enjoy Streaming

    Once connected, you can start streaming your content to the selected device.
