How to Connect

Connecting Your Device

Follow these steps to connect your device and start using our app:

  1. Step 1: Open the App

    Launch the app on your device and navigate to the connection settings.

  2. Step 2: Select Your Device

    Choose the device you want to connect to from the list of available devices.

  3. Step 3: Confirm Connection

    Confirm the connection request on both your device and the chosen device.

  4. Step 4: Start Streaming

    Once connected, you can start streaming content from your device to the chosen device.
