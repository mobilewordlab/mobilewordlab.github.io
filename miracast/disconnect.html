How to Disconnect Your Device

To disconnect your device from the application, follow these simple steps:

  1. Step 1: Open the App

    Launch the app on your device and go to the connection settings.

  2. Step 2: Access Connected Devices

    Find the list of connected devices within the app settings.

  3. Step 3: Select the Device

    Choose the device you want to disconnect from the list.

  4. Step 4: Confirm Disconnection

    Confirm the disconnection request to remove the device from the app.
